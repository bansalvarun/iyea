Research

The Indian Youth Economic Association accepts academic papers, reviews and critique pieces for publication in its online platform The Agenda. Interdisciplinary research is encouraged while papers solely dealing with the following fields may also be accepted:

  1. Economics

  2. Finance

  3. Political Science

  4. International Relations

  5. History

  6. Law

Selected candidates will be provided research materials and guided through the process with a constant feedback and review process by the mentor(s).
