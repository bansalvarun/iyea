Research

The Indian Youth Economic Association is accepting academic papers for publication in its Journal of Economics, Social Sciences & History. Interdisciplinary research is encouraged while papers solely dealing with the following fields will also be accepted:

  1. Economics

  2. Finance

  3. Psychology

  4. Sociology

  5. Political Science

  6. International Relations

  7. History

The Editorial Board consists of undergraduate and post-graduate scholars while a Panel of Advisers consisting of faculty members from various academic institutions will guide them.
