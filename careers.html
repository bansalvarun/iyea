Internship Program

INDIAN YOUTH ECONOMIC ASSOCIATION

The Indian Youth Economic Association is pleased to announce its Internship Program for Winter 2018-19. Internships are available for both undergraduate and postgraduate students.


Currently available positions are as follows:

Research Associates

Graduate and undergraduate students with experience in research in Economics, Finance, Political Science, International Relations, History, Law or Technology may apply for this program. Field work may be required based on the projects taken up/assigned to the associate. Research work will be directed towards our online news platform The Agenda.

Logistics Associates:

Individuals with professional experience in management and logistics for events are encouraged to apply for this program. Interns will work on upcoming events & conferences collaborating with national and/or international partners. Stipend will be performance-based.

Interested parties may apply by sending in their resumes to interns.iyea@gmail.com
