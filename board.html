Board of Trustees

Ajay Sabharwal is the President of the Indian Youth Economic Association and in this capacity has been shaping its vision and heading its operations for the last six years. A graduate in Economics and History, Ajay is currently pursuing Law from the Faculty of Law, Delhi University.


Ajay Sabharwal

Madhav Bharat Bhushan serves as Vice President, Finance. He completed his Bachelors in Management Studies from Delhi University and his Postgraduation from Duke. Madhav is currently studying to be a Chartered Financial Analyst. He is astute with finances and has helped the trust organize large conferences in the most efficient ways.


Madhav Bharat Bhushan

Sarthak Garg is Vice President for Logistics & Liaison. In the last year of his Chartered Accountancy Programme, Sarthak and has been a key asset to the IYEA. Having formerly served as the Head of the North Division and Treasurer of the IYEA, he now facilitates dayto-day operations and focuses on the expansion of the organization in different parts of the country.


Sarthak Garg

Ashita Alag has served as the Secretary of the IYEA since its inception and is now serving as the Treasurer as well. A student of Law and a humanitarian, Ashita has been the face of the social agendas & programmes of the IYEA. Ashita has been a silent and self-less work horse, having served as the backbone of the organization since 2012.


Ashita Alag

Neoma Pasricha serves as a Trustee of the IYEA. She completed her postgraduation in Finance from the London School of Economics and has an in-depth understanding of Accountancy and Economics, as well. She has researched extensively in these areas and has published several research papers. Working as an executive in the banking sector, Neoma has been guiding the Indian Youth Economic Association since its inception.


Neoma Dhingra

Sakshi Khanna serves as a Trustee of the IYEA. She completed her Bachelors in Chemical Engineering & Masters in Business Administration (MBA) degree and is an entrepreneur. Her business interests lie in the cosmetics industry, while she also keeps many social causes close to her heart.


Sakshi Khanna
