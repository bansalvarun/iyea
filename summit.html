The Summit on War & Politics


4-6 November, 2016


India Habitat Centre & Institute for Defence Studies and Analyses, New Delhi

The Indian Youth Economic Association and organized the Summit on War & Politics in collaboration with the Indian Debating League on 5 th and 6 th November 2016 at the India Habitat Centre and the PHD Chamber of Commerce & Industry. The event was sponsored by INFRES Methodex Pvt. Ltd. Academic simulations such as the European Great Powers, Indian Independence Movement, the German War Cabinet, Donald Trump’s and Hillary Clinton’s Campaign Committees were organized. Mr. Deepak Verma, President of the Indian Debating Union and chief mentor to the conference was the Guest of Honour while Mr. Vivek Kataria was the Chief Guest.
